Larry Bird (1979-1992)

Larry Bird played with the Celtics for 13 seasons. He scored 21,791 over those seasons. He won 3 NBA Championships and 2 NBA Finals MVP awards

Alex Wong/NSN/Getty Images Link

Bill Russel (1956-1969)

Bill Russel played 13 seasons with the Celtics and coached for 3. He won 11 NBA Championships during his career at the celtics and scored 14,522 points.

Dick Raphael/NBAE/Getty Images Link

John Havlicek(1962–1978)

John Havlicek played 16 seasons for the Celtics. Over those seasons he amassed 26,395 points and is the Celtics' all-time leader in points. Havlicek won 8 NBA Championships and 1 MVP award.
